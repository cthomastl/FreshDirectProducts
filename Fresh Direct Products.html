Our Story

FreshDirect was founded by four entrepreneurs who shared a vision: to eliminate the barriers between quality food suppliers and customers. Frustrated by the limitations of traditional distribution models, Mehak, Chris, Alex, and Tyler combined their expertise in operations, technology, and finance to build a platform that puts customers first.

Today, FreshDirect serves thousands of customers across the region, delivering premium quality food with unmatched flexibility and transparency. Our cloud-based platform enables direct ordering, customizable logistics, and real-time tracking‚Äîtransforming how food reaches your table.
